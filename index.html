About Us

About Our Restaurant

It serves Palestinian dishes.

The food service offers a variety of options, including Palestinian dishes and fast food, to cater to dormitory students who live far from home. Ideally situated in close proximity to the students, this convenient service saves them time that would otherwise be spent cooking or searching for food, allowing them more time to focus on their studies.

  • We offer comfortable sessions inspired by traditional Palestinian gatherings.
  • We serve fresh locally sourced food.
  • Special dietary requests can also be accommodated upon request.
